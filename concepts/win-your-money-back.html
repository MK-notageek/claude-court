WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKThe Problem

The standard refund attracts the wrong behavior.

“Satisfaction guaranteed” converts skeptics but it also invites people who consume passively and exit whenever the going gets uncomfortable.

Worse: the passive guarantee does nothing for motivation. The buyer paid, received access, and can quit at any time with no consequence. It serves the sale but poisons the outcome.

The client who does not complete the work does not get the result. And then they ask for the refund. You lose the money and the testimonial.

02
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKDefined

Pay to play. Earn to keep.

Win-Your-Money-Back is a conditional refund mechanism attached to completion, not satisfaction. The client pays full price. If they complete the defined steps — attendance, assignments, milestone gates — they get their money back.

The refund is not the outcome. The result is. The refund is proof that the client did the work. And if they did the work, they almost certainly got the result — and they usually choose to keep the program rather than claim the cash.

WYMB is not a liability. It is a commitment device. The program designer sets the milestones; the client activates them by paying. Both parties now want the same thing.

03
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKThe Three Types

Three ways to structure it.

Not every business suits the same model. The right structure depends on whether your outcome is binary or gradual, and how measurable your milestones are.

All three share a core rule: the conditions you set must be the same conditions your best clients already meet. Engineer for success, not for edge-case refund exclusion.

04
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKBoth Sides

When both parties have skin in the game, everyone works harder.

The classic risk-shift says: “I believe in this enough to refund you.” WYMB goes further: we both have something to lose, so let’s both make sure you win.

Clients who invest more tend to commit more. The upfront payment is an activation energy — once paid, the tendency to follow through rises sharply. WYMB keeps it high all the way to the end.

Their investment drives their follow-through. Your guarantee drives your delivery.
05
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKThe Accountability Engine

Build accountability into the structure.

The milestone conditions are not barriers — they are accountability mechanisms. Each gate forces the client to do what works. Doing what works produces results.

Design your milestones by studying your best clients: what did they all do in week 1? Week 2? Those actions are your conditions. Copy what works; make it compulsory.

Make the milestones achievable by anyone willing to show up. If a motivated client cannot hit the conditions, your conditions are wrong — not your clients.

06
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKThe Flywheel

Winners become your best ads.

When a client earns refund eligibility, they have done something remarkable: they completed a hard thing and got a real result. That story is a better ad than any testimonial you could ever write yourself.

Most clients at completion do not want the refund. They’d rather keep the result. But the fact that they could have claimed it — and chose not to — is the most powerful social proof you own.

Every cohort that runs through WYMB produces a new tier of proof: completion rates, refund waiver rates, and documented outcomes that your next cohort can read before buying.

07
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKDesign It

If your conditions are right, refunds are rare.

Over-engineer the conditions and clients feel trapped. Under-engineer them and everyone claims the refund regardless. The sweet spot: conditions a motivated client can complete, which also track to your actual deliverable.

Look at your refund claims. Are they from clients who completed? Rarely — because completion produces results, and results end the complaint. If refunds spike, audit the milestones first, not the clients.

✗“Attend one call and request a refund any time within 30 days.” — Too easy; every undecided buyer claims it.
✓“Complete all 6 weekly exercises and the final audit — we return your full investment.” — Achievable, accountable, rare to claim.
08
WIN-YOUR-MONEY-BACKThe Math

The math always works.

If 20% more people buy because of the guarantee and 5% more claim a refund, the net is +15% revenue. On 100 clients at $2,000: without WYMB = $200K. With WYMB = $240K sales − $10K refunds = $230K net — plus better results, more testimonials, and higher LTV.

✗“If you’re unsatisfied, just let us know and we’ll sort it out.” — Vague. Passive. No skin in the game.
✓“Complete all six milestones and we return your investment. Most of our graduates choose to keep it.”
